From Clement Town
to Your Kitchen

Matasree Super Industries was founded with a vision to provide families with premium, pure spices and masalas that enhance the authentic taste of every dish. What began as a small operation in Clement Town, Dehradun, has grown into a trusted name across India.

For over 18 years, we have been committed to sourcing the finest raw materials, using traditional grinding techniques, and maintaining the highest quality standards. Every product is lab-tested for purity and safety.
